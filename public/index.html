Hoy! Pinoy!

Hello there, welcome to our page! We are a cultural club on campus that welcomes everyone interested in the Filipino culture. We act as a resource that informs and celebrates the traditional and modern parts of the Philippines. We've done outreach to the Filipino community in Massachusetts, including the Filipino School, Philippine dance companies, related festivals, and other cultural organizations. We're also part of District 1, a larger community of the Filipino college clubs all over the Boston area, so we're never in shortage of connections! We host regular meetings and events that aim to create a friendly environment where we can all come together and find some refuge from the day-to-day of school. Come on by and learn about what we're all about!
